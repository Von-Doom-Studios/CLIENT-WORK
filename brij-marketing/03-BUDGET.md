## Rules

- **Monthly cap:** set by `[client]`. No overrun without approval.
- **Per-campaign cap:** no campaign over 40% of monthly cap without `[client]` sign-off. See [05-CAMPAIGNS.md](./05-CAMPAIGNS.md).
- **Boost floor:** organic post must clear 2x baseline before paid spend.
- **ROI threshold:** track cost-per-follower and cost-per-email. If a campaign comes in 3x baseline organic cost for 2 months running, pause.
- **Kill switch:** if total paid is producing <1 follower per $5 or <1 email per $10 across a full month, pause paid.
