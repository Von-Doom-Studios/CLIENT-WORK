## LEADING INDICATORS

### Email Marketing Health
| Metric | Target | Red Flag | Action If Red |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| Open Rate | >30% | <25% | A/B test subject lines |
| Click Rate | >5% | <3% | Review email content and CTAs |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <2% | >3% | Reduce frequency, improve segmentation |
| List Growth Rate | >5%/week | <2%/week | Add more lead magnets |

### Social Media Health  
| Metric | Target | Red Flag | Action If Red |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| Engagement Rate | >3% | <2% | Review content pillars |
| Profile Clicks | >2% of impressions | <1% | Optimize bio and pinned content |
| Follower Growth | >50/week | <20/week | Increase posting frequency |
| Mention Volume | >10/week | <5/week | More community engagement |

### Viral Loop Health
| Metric | Target | Red Flag | Action If Red |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| Viral Coefficient | >0.3 | <0.2 | Increase referral rewards |
| Referrer Activation | >30% | <20% | Improve referral UX |
| Referred Conversion | >15% | <10% | Better onboarding for referred users |
| Time to First Referral | <48 hours | >72 hours | Earlier referral prompts |

## DAILY TRACKING (Automated)

### Dashboard Metrics (Updated Every Morning)
```
Email Performance:
- New signups: [X] (target: 15/day by Month 2)
- Yesterday's open rate: [X]% (target: >30%)
- Active sequences: [X] users in nurture flows

Social Media:
- X followers: [X] (+/- from yesterday)
- Best performing post: [content] ([X] impressions)
- Mentions requiring response: [X]

Product Metrics:
- Flight searches: [X] (API calls)
- Bookings completed: [X] 
- Referrals generated: [X]
- Revenue: $[X] (bookings - referral costs)
```

### Red Flag Alerts
Auto-alerts sent to team if:
- Email open rate drops below 25% for 3 consecutive sends
- Social engagement rate below 2% for 5 consecutive posts  
- No new bookings for 48 hours
- Referral system showing errors

## WEEKLY REVIEW (Every Friday)

### Performance Analysis
- **Best content:** What got highest engagement? Why?
- **Worst content:** What flopped? What can we learn?
- **Channel performance:** Which platforms drove most signups/bookings?
- **Referral insights:** Who referred most? What motivated them?

### Optimization Actions
- **A/B test results:** What tests concluded? What should we implement?
- **Content adjustments:** What formats should we do more/less of?
- **Technical fixes:** Any system issues need addressing?
- **Budget reallocation:** What ad spend should shift based on performance?

### Next Week Planning
- **Content calendar:** 7 days of posts planned and approved
- **Email schedule:** Which sequences send when?
- **Tests to run:** What will we A/B test next?
- **Outreach targets:** Who should we engage with?

## COMPETITIVE BENCHMARKING

### Monthly Comparison (Research-Based)
- **Crypto startups:** Average 2.1% email open rate decline - are we maintaining 30%+?
- **AI tools:** Average 1,200 signups/month for new products - are we hitting 2,500 by Month 3?
- **SaaS referrals:** Best programs achieve 0.4-0.6 viral coefficient - are we trending toward 0.5?
- **Social growth:** Top B2B accounts grow 300-500 followers/month - are we hitting targets?

### Feature Parity Checks
- **Every 2 weeks:** What are competitors launching? Do we need to respond?
- **Every month:** Are their referral programs better than ours?
- **Every quarter:** Have they found channels we're not using effectively?

## SUCCESS DIAGNOSTICS

### If Email Growth Is Slow
**Likely causes:** 
- Lead magnets not valuable enough
- Landing page conversion issues
- Organic traffic too low

**Tests to run:**
- New lead magnet with broader appeal
- Landing page copy A/B tests  
- SEO investment or paid traffic

### If Social Growth Is Slow
**Likely causes:**
- Content not engaging enough
- Wrong posting times
- Not engaging with community enough

**Tests to run:**
- Different content formats (more video?)
- Posting time experiments
- More active community participation

### If Bookings Are Low
**Likely causes:**
- Product not ready (technical issues)
- Price point wrong
- Trust issues

**Tests to run:**
- Technical debugging
- Pricing experiments  
- More social proof and testimonials

### If Referrals Aren't Working
**Likely causes:**
- Rewards not compelling enough
- UX too complicated
- Users don't see value in sharing

**Tests to run:**
- Increase reward values
- Simplify sharing flow
- Add social proof ("1,247 people have earned credits")

## FAILURE CONDITIONS

### When to Pivot Strategy
- **Month 2:** If email signups <300, completely different lead magnets needed
- **Month 3:** If social followers <800, channel mix needs major shift  
- **Month 6:** If bookings <500, product-market fit issues

### When to Increase Investment
- **Viral coefficient >0.4:** Double down on referral program
- **Email open rate >40%:** Increase email frequency
- **Social engagement >5%:** Increase posting frequency + paid amplification

### When to Scale Team
- **Month 4:** If metrics trending toward goals, consider full-time marketing hire
- **Month 6:** If exceeding goals, consider marketing manager + specialist roles

**The metrics drive decisions, not instincts.**
