## Weekly Boost Budget Allocation

$200/week. Allocated based on content performance:

- **$75–100** on the week's anchor content (launch thread, major announcement)
- **$25–50** on the best-performing organic tweet from the week (boost what's already working)
- **$25–50** reserved for engagement/reply boosts or weekend content
- **Rule:** If a boosted post gets <1% engagement rate after 24 hours, kill it and reallocate.

---

## Weekly Review (Every Friday)

1. What did we post this week?
2. What got the most engagement? (impressions, clicks, replies, bookmarks)
3. What flopped?
4. How much did we spend? What was the return?
5. What's the plan for next week?

Log results in this doc (append to bottom) or in a separate weekly log.
