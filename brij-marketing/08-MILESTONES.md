## Email Milestones (Detailed)

| Phase | Cumulative Signups | Sends/Week | Key Tactic |
|-------|--------------------|------------|------------|
| Phase 1 | 500 | 0–1 | Capture on brij.fi + premium access signups |
| Phase 2 | 2,500 | 1 | Weekly updates + lead magnet + wallet launch blast |
| Phase 3 | **10,000** | 1–2 | Welcome sequences + referral program + post-booking flows |

### Email Growth Levers
1. **Homepage capture** — above-the-fold on brij.fi
2. **Travel flow capture** — post-search or pre-booking on travel.brij.fi
3. **Premium access** — email required for premium tier
4. **Lead magnet** — "AI Travel Guide" gated PDF
5. **Referral** — "Give a friend $5" program in Phase 3
6. **Exit intent** — popup on travel.brij.fi for Phase 3
7. **Post-booking share** — social share prompt drives new traffic → capture

## The Anti-Vanity Rule

Do not celebrate metrics that feel good but don't move the needle.

- 10,000 impressions with 0 clicks = seen but not compelling
- 500 followers from a giveaway who never engage = empty
- A viral tweet that drives traffic to a page with no CTA = wasted

The only metrics that matter connect to the milestones above. Everything else is context, not achievement.
