# 06 - CAMPAIGNS: Brij Marketing

Campaign architecture for launches and milestones. Each campaign has an objective, dates, audience, channels, assets, owner, and success metrics. Numbered in order of activation.

**Owner:** VD-Marketing
**Related files:** [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md), [03-BUDGET.md](./03-BUDGET.md), [05-CALENDAR.md](./05-CALENDAR.md), [07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md](./07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md)
**Last updated:** 2026-05-22

## Campaign anatomy (every campaign uses this)

- **Name and number**
- **Objective:** what business outcome this drives
- **Dates:** start, end, key milestones in between
- **Audience:** which of the 4 from [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md) is primary
- **Channels:** X, LinkedIn, YouTube, email, partner
- **Assets:** posts, visuals, landing pages, emails (linked to [07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md](./07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md))
- **Owner:** lead person/agent
- **Success metrics:** what we measure and the threshold for success
- **Budget:** how much paid spend (linked to [03-BUDGET.md](./03-BUDGET.md))
- **Retro:** post-campaign review with what worked / what didn't / what we'd do differently

## Campaign 01 - Espresso Cash is now Brij Smart Wallet

**Status:** active. Week 1.

**Objective:** Establish @brij_fi as the continuation of Espresso Cash with a new mission. Convert existing Espresso Cash followers into Brij followers. Plant the flag that the wallet you've been hearing about is now agent-shaped.

**Dates:** 2026-05-25 (announcement post) to 2026-06-07 (campaign wrap, retro logged).

**Audience:** primarily existing followers (audience 2 and 3 from [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md)). Secondary: audience 1 (agent infra builders) seeing the post via amplification.

**Channels:**
- X: pinned announcement + 2 follow-up posts (week 1 and week 2 in [05-CALENDAR.md](./05-CALENDAR.md))
- LinkedIn: 1 founder post explaining the transition
- Email: announcement to any existing Espresso Cash email list if one is recoverable (Anthony confirm)
- Site: brij.fi banner or hero update (brief to Dev)

**Assets:**
- X post 1 (pinned): announcement post - copy in [07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md#post-w1-x-1](./07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md)
- X post 2: smart wallet feature drumbeat that reinforces the conversion - [07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md#post-w1-x-3](./07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md)
- LinkedIn post: founder POV - [07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md#post-w1-li-1](./07-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md)
- Visual: side-by-side or transition graphic (Espresso wordmark → Brij wordmark). Brief to Creative.

**Owner:** VD-Marketing drafts. Founders post manually. Creative produces visual. Ops handles any partner amplification asks (Solana ecosystem accounts, x402 community).

**Success metrics:**
- Pinned post: minimum 50 likes, 10 reposts, 5,000 impressions in first 48 hours
- Net follower gain: +50 in campaign window (baseline organic is ~10-20/week per [02-STRATEGY.md](./02-STRATEGY.md))
- 1 quote-post or repost from an ecosystem account (Solana, Coinbase devrel, x402 community)
- Email list (if recoverable): >40% open rate, >5% click on brij.fi link

**Budget:** boost the pinned post if it clears 2x baseline engagement by 24h. See [03-BUDGET.md](./03-BUDGET.md).

**Retro:** logged in [11-ANALYTICS.md](./11-ANALYTICS.md) by 2026-06-08.

## Campaign 02 - First YouTube upload (flight booking demo)

**Status:** queued. Week 4 start.

**Objective:** Establish YouTube as a discovery surface and prove the product visually. A 30-60 second clip of an agent booking a flight is the single most thumb-stopping asset we have.

**Dates:** 2026-06-15 (upload) to 2026-06-21 (campaign wrap).

**Audience:** all 4. Demo crosses audiences.

**Channels:** YouTube (upload + Shorts cut), X (clip embed), LinkedIn (clip cross-post), email (newsletter issue 01 features it).

**Assets:**
- 30-60s edited clip (Creative produces, VD-Marketing scripts the voiceover/captions)
- YouTube long-form description with timestamps + brij.fi link
- X post with native upload of clip
- LinkedIn cross-post with founder commentary
- Newsletter issue 01 lead

**Owner:** VD-Marketing scripts. Creative produces. Founders post manually. Detailed in [10-YOUTUBE.md](./10-YOUTUBE.md).

**Success metrics:**
- 1,000 YouTube views in first 7 days
- 100 net X followers from clip post + boost
- 50 emails from clip-driven traffic to brij.fi
- 5 inbound DMs/replies from builders asking technical questions

**Budget:** YouTube pre-roll + X boost. Caps per [03-BUDGET.md](./03-BUDGET.md).

**Retro:** logged in [11-ANALYTICS.md](./11-ANALYTICS.md) by 2026-06-22.
