## Rules of engagement

1. **Replies must add value.** A code snippet, a counter-example, an extension of the point with brij as the proof. Empty "great post!" replies are banned.
2. **Founder-voiced, not brand-voiced.** Replies from @brij_fi are fine but the highest-leverage replies come from the founder's personal account quote-posting or replying with technical specifics.
3. **15-20 minutes/day on X.** Not a one-off task. Daily ritual.
4. **Quality over volume.** 3 substantive replies a day beats 30 empty ones.
5. **Quote-post selectively.** Use quote-posts when we have a sharp extension of their point. Otherwise reply in-thread.
6. **No DMs without permission.** Engagement is public. Outreach is Ops territory.

## Weekly engagement plan

**Monday (lead time):** scan Tier 1 accounts for posts worth replying to. Aim for 1-2 substantive engagements.

**Tuesday-Thursday (build relationships):** rotate through Tier 2 (Mon/Wed) and Tier 3 (Tue/Thu). Daily target: 3-5 substantive replies across the rotation.

**Friday (capitalize):** Tier 1 + Tier 4. If a piece of media or a high-reach account is talking about anything in our space, weigh in.

**Daily (always):** 15-20 minutes of in-thread engagement is the floor. Founder time, ideally.
