# Ad Plan — BRIJ

$200/week boost budget. Every dollar tracked.

---

## Budget Structure

**Weekly total:** $200
**Monthly total:** ~$800

| Allocation | Amount | Purpose |
|------------|--------|---------|
| Anchor content boost | $75–100 | The week's main post (launch thread, demo video, announcement) |
| Best organic performer | $50–75 | Boost whichever tweet got the most organic traction that week |
| Engagement/test | $25–50 | Try new angles, weekend content, or reply-chain boosts |

---

## Phase 1: X Only (Weeks 1–4)

BRIJ's audience is on X. No other paid platform justified until Phase 2.

### Campaign 1: Espresso Cash → BRIJ Rebrand (Week 1)

| Creative | Format | Budget | Targeting |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Rebrand thread (Thread #1 from Content Bank) | Promoted tweet | $50 | Followers of Solana ecosystem accounts, AI/crypto interests |
| Travel teaser ("Monday, your agent books its first flight") | Promoted tweet | $25 | Same + travel interests |

**Expected:** 10K–30K impressions, 100–300 link clicks, 50–100 profile visits

### Campaign 2: Travel Launch (Week 2)

| Creative | Format | Budget | Targeting |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Launch thread (Thread #2) — first tweet | Promoted tweet | $75 | AI/agent builders, Solana devs, crypto-native users |
| "No tracking" standalone tweet | Promoted tweet | $25 | Same + frequent travelers |

**Expected:** 20K–50K impressions, 200–500 link clicks, 100+ profile visits

### Campaign 3: Momentum (Weeks 3–4)

| Creative | Format | Budget | Targeting |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Best-performing organic tweet from the week | Promoted tweet | $50–75/week | Informed by Week 2 analytics |
| Results thread or user spotlight | Promoted tweet | $25–50/week | Same |

---

## Rules

1. **Never boost content with <1% organic engagement rate.** If it didn't work organically, money won't fix it.
2. **Kill underperformers within 48 hours.** Reallocate budget to what's working.
3. **Track every dollar.** Weekly log: what was boosted, how much, what it returned (impressions, clicks, conversions).
4. **CPA must stay below booking margin by Phase 3.** If paid acquisition costs more than the revenue it generates, stop and fix the funnel.
